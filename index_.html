Moral Distress Thermometer

Capstone project for Lucia D. Wocial, School of Nursing

Dr. Lucia aims in providing a healthy environment where critical care nurses make their optimal contributions to patients and families. In this attempt they are looking to develop a website which will enable the nurses to record the level of distress they have been experiencing during the past week.

About Me



Hi There! I'm Manali - User Experience Designer and a Frontend Developer. Soon to graduate (May 2017) Master of Science program in Human Computer Interaction from Indiana University, Indianapolis. I am actively looking for full time positions as a User Experience Designer.

I like minimalist design approach of iOS designs as well as intuitive designs of Andriod. Can't to wait to get my hands dirty in designing for Wearable Devices and Augmented Reality. My domain interests are Healthcare and Hospitality. I believe there is a lot of data lying around for people to give it meaning with data visualization. To keep my brain sharp, I practice frontend development skills by taking up development work in the COMET Lab work while working as a Research Assistant.
