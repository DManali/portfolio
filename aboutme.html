About Me

Hi, I'm Manali - A User Experience Designer and a Frontend Developer. Graduate student of Human Computer Interaction from Indiana University, Indianapolis. I am actively looking for full-time positions as a User Experience Designer/User Researcher.

I like minimalist design approach of iOS designs as well as intuitive designs of Andriod. Can't wait to get my hands dirty in designing for Wearable Devices and Augmented Reality. I believe there is a lot of data lying around for people to give it a meaning with data visualization. So that makes it another area of my interest. To keep my brain sharp, I practice frontend development skills by taking up development work in the Convergent Media and Exploratory Technology Lab as a Research Assistant.
