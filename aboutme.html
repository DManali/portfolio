About Me


Hi, I'm Manali - A User Experience Designer and a Frontend Developer. Graduate student of Human Computer Interaction from Indiana University, Indianapolis. I am actively looking for full-time positions as a User Experience Designer/User Researcher. Throughout my class projects, summer internship and as a Student Research Assistant I was able to perform all of the roles required for user experience designing. As UX Design Best practices, I have learned and followed iOS Human Interface Guidelines.

I like minimalist design approach of iOS designs as well as intuitive designs of Andriod. I know it is a tough task to achieve both in your designs. However, I strive to achieve it. Can't wait to get my hands dirty in designing for Wearable Devices and Augmented Reality. I believe there is a lot of data lying around for people to give it meaning with data visualization. So that is another are of my interest. To keep my brain sharp, I practice frontend development skills by taking up development work in the COMET Lab work while working as a Research Assistant.
