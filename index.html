Design Projects - Contribution towards HCI classes, different ways of solving problems of different domains

iMOD

Capstone project for Lucia D. Wocial, School of Nursing

Dr. Lucia aims in providing a healthy environment where critical care nurses make their optimal contributions to patients and families. In this attempt they are looking to develop a website which will enable the nurses to record the level of distress they have been experiencing during the past week.

Which movie should I watch tonight?

Final project for Interactive visual analytics class

A question that almost all of us face on Friday evenings. Utilized the class learnings to evaluate two of the movie visualizations presented in the "Visualize Hollywood Challenge" by "Information is Beautiful" and we proposed a new design apparoch to search a movie to watch based on the User study

TABLES

Final project of Prototyping Class - For MAC Lab

Tutor Assisted Browser-based Learning Environment for Students (TABLES) is a collaborative online synchronous learning center for learning assistance. Our proposed project is intended to recreate a MATH tutor center which provides guidance and assistance to students on mathematics concepts.

Open Journal System

For IUPUI University library Journals

Understanding and managing the flow of Journals in the process of publishing was a tedious task for the Journal Editors with the help of the existing Journal website built using OJS Framework. We provided a solution based on Kanban board workflow to optimize Journal Editor's Journal Flow
